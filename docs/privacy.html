Privacy Policy

Speed Lab Jr.

Effective date: March 25, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 25, 2026

The short version: Speed Lab Jr. does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. All your data stays on your device.

What We Collect

Speed Lab Jr. collects no personal information whatsoever. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data sent off-device
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to us
  • Any information entered into the app

How Your Data Is Stored

All workout logs, progress data, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

Speed Lab Jr. does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services. There are no SDKs, trackers, or external APIs that receive data about you or your usage.

Children's Privacy

Speed Lab Jr. is designed for use by young athletes, including children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no personal information at all, it is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's guidelines for apps directed at children.

No account creation is required. No data is transmitted from the device.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Because we collect no data, any changes would only ever expand, not reduce, user privacy protections.

Contact
